Bat Company

Laserbeam Productions present you: Bat Company. The newest endless flyer available in the Google Play store for phones and tablets!

Ever wondered what it’s like to be the one and only Vladimir Dracula? Well, you won’t cuz you a bat. #batlife. But still a vampire - bat. And still Dracula… just not as a human… vampire.

You, as Vladi the vampire bat, will have to survive the cold, dark, and dangerous caves of Transylvania. And, as the bat that you are, you have to use your echo location to navigate and avoid obstacles, obtain blood, boosters, and pick-up power-ups.

Features of the game

  • Unique endless runner were vision is key
  • Full Google Play integration
  • Achievements
  • Leaderboards
  • Fun and retro feeling
